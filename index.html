Welcome to I2I website!



Idea to Innovation is a club started by the students of Institute of Aeronautical Engineering in the year 2021 to evoke students’ creativity levels by encouraging them to take-up projects with varying levels of complexities. This Club aims at taking up various innovative ideas and methodologies of the students’ and help them execute it by providing technical assistance backed by eminent faculty support. The club mainly focuses on critical and competitive problem-solving skills by inculcating group discussions and effective learning techniques. At I2I we believe practical and interactive way of learning will boost and enhance the creative and logical thinking levels of the students who aim at achieving high in the field of their passion and interests.
