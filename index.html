Welcome to I2I website!



Idea to Innovation is a club started by the students of Institute of Aeronautical Engineering in the year 2021 to evoke students’ creativity levels by encouraging them to take-up projects with varying levels of complexities. This Club aims at taking up various innovative ideas and methodologies of the students’ and help them execute it by providing technical assistance backed by eminent faculty support. The club mainly focuses on critical and competitive problem-solving skills by inculcating group discussions and effective learning techniques. At I2I we believe practical and interactive way of learning will boost and enhance the creative and logical thinking levels of the students who aim at achieving high in the field of their passion and interests.

About Us

Unlike ever before, computer science engineers are addressing the most acute global and social issues impacting our future. Through cutting edge research, innovations, and multi-disciplinary collaborations, addressing challenges has become the most significant part of the real world. Idea to Innovation promises to play an integral role in shaping your ideas for these future developments. The motto Think – Innovate – Achieve is what that keeps the torch of the club, Idea to Innovation burning.
I2I is a lively, inclusive community of students and faculty, conducting various events and activities to develop critical thinking and problem- solving skills, as well as hands-on learning. Innovation is central to our mission. Our aim for innovation focus is grounded in the strengths of our supportive team-mates and faculty who are sure to guide and assist in the fields of Competitive programming, Web-development (front-end, back-end and server-side), Mobile-based applications, Photoshop and much more..



Eagerly waiting for all those students with interactive, inquisitive, and innovative minds to be a part of the I2I club and get “your Ideas turned into Innovations”.

--Team I2I
